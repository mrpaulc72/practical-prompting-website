## Notes

### Design Decisions
- **Single-page design:** Maintains focus on primary CTA
- **No navigation menu:** Prevents distraction from conversion path
- **Minimal JavaScript:** Ensures fast load times and accessibility
- **Mobile-first CSS:** Optimizes for 60%+ mobile traffic

### Technical Choices
- **Vanilla JavaScript:** Avoids framework bloat
- **No build tools:** Simplifies deployment and maintenance
- **Inline SVGs:** Ensures icons load immediately
- **Google Fonts:** Professional typography without hosting costs
