## üéØ Strategic Overview

### The Problem We're Solving
The previous homepage suffered from a **"strategic fracture"** ‚Äî it lacked focus and failed to validate the core frustration of our target audience: inconsistent AI results that waste time and money.

### Our Solution
A laser-focused, single-purpose homepage that acts as a **"magnifying glass"** for the brand. Every element is designed to:
1. **Validate** the visitor's frustration with AI
2. **Present** a systematic solution (the CLO Framework)
3. **Convert** visitors to the primary sales page
